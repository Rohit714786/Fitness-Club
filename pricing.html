Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is there a joining fee?

No. There are absolutely no hidden fees or joining charges. Your first payment covers your first full month.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment required. Cancel with 7 days notice before your next billing date.

Do you offer student or corporate discounts?

Yes! We offer 15% off for students with valid ID and 20% off for corporate groups of 5 or more. Contact us for details.

How does the free trial work?

New members get their first month free on the Pro plan. No credit card required upfront. After 30 days, choose to continue or cancel — no questions asked.
