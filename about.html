FitZone was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to world-class fitness. Since 2009, we've been the home of champions, beginners, and everyone in between.

Founded 2009

THE VISION BEHIND FITZONE

It all started in a small garage studio in 2009. Our founder, Marcus Steele, a former professional athlete, wanted to create a space where every person — regardless of fitness level or background — could train like an elite athlete.

Over 15 years, FitZone has grown into a flagship fitness destination, spanning 25,000 sq ft of premium training space, housing cutting-edge equipment, recovery suites, and a community of over 5,000 passionate members.
